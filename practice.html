If you would like to play a practice match with a referee, you can use this system. The form can be filled out with the link in step 1 or at the bottom of this page. Here's how it works:

  1. Students play the match

  2. The "referee" (such as another student or coach) fills out this easy form on their smartphone or tablet (computer will work, too but it's not recommended)

  3. On the first page of the form, enter the email address of a student or coach who would like to receive the score report

  4. Check your email to look for "FLL Score Report - Team ####" from jhasenklein@gmail.com

  5. Review your score report –– The score report is a detailed explanation of which matches you scored points in and how many points you earned total
